Return Policy

At Pink Boss, we want you to love your fragrance purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery for a full refund, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging. Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will notify you about the status of your refund. Approved refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 5-7 business days. Note that shipping costs are non-fundable, and return shipping is the customer's responsibility, and return shipping is the customer's responsibility unless the return is due to an error on our part. For any questions or assistance with a return, please reach out to our customer support team via our contact page. Thank you for choosing Pink Boss, and we are here to help make your experience as seamless as possible.
